Jeff Peters

About

Jeff Peters is a Registered Nurse who works in a critical care unit for a hospital in Nebraska. He graduates with two degrees. The first one is a bachelor’s degree in art in Psychology with minor pre-med, and a bachelor’s degree in science in Nursing.

He was planning on medical school. However, while taking a job as a nurse aide, he got to experience a day in the life of a doctor. He saw how incredible busy doctors were and how they didn’t have time for family or personal life. This event helped Jeff decided to become a registered nurse. He wanted to be a person who can spend time with his family and be a great dad to his kid. Jeff enjoys being a registered nurse because he can help people by maintaining and improving their health. He enjoys many hobbies such as shooting, scuba diving, camping, reading, photography, and skydiving. He enjoys shooting rifles and shotguns. He has completed a practical defensive shooting course. He also used to shoot in competitions around the country.

He enjoys shooting because it’s a sport that requires mental discipline and physical strength. He also enjoys scuba diving. The air we breathe is 79 nitrogen and 21 percent oxygen. Its physical activity that you can see something totally different from normal world. Jeff enjoys travelling to different countries to do scuba diving. The countries he has visited to scuba diving are, US, Mexico, Belize, Honduras, Guatemala, and Barbados.

When he was a kid, he wanted to be an astronaut because he has always been interested in space. His favorite vacation is the one where he went on a road trip with his family to Arizona. He is a dedicated Registered Nurse, who loves his work and enjoys spending time with his family. He has a good career and is very proud to work for almost 30 years in the same place.
